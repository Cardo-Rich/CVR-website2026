Cardo's pill-shaped call-to-action button; use `primary` (rose) for the single most important action on a view, `ink` for dark/secondary CTAs, and `ghost` on cream or photo surfaces.

```jsx
<Button variant="primary" size="lg" iconEnd="→">Get my estimate</Button>
<Button variant="ghost">Learn more</Button>
```

Variants: `primary` (rose, default), `ink`, `ghost`, `ghost-light` (for dark/photo backgrounds). Sizes: `sm | md | lg`. Pass `block` for full width and `as="a"` to render a link.
